Webcomic Itch.io Patreon

I'm an amateur artist and game designer, and a hobbyist storyteller. My current major work is the Arcane Adventures Webcomic, coming up on 7 years. On the side, I'm also working on a couple of connected projects into my own little storyverse, because it's fun, and a little scary(?)!

Arcane Adventures Webcomic

An alternate telling of a story from a cancelled game, featuring the amnesiac prodigy Juniper in her quest against those who seek to disrupt peace within The Seven Seas while finding her purpose in her new life. Currently the longest running project, Juniper's adventure is currently nearing the end, approaching the doorstep of a thousand-year nemesis.

Creative Freedom

The rise of a foolish, hungry little painter and a faithful little critter in a colorless world feasted upon by illogical and outside entities that seek to attain realism. This'll be a 2D Roguelike Deckbuilder with other artistic gameplay outside of combat that'll aid you in your journey to stop any outside entities in any way Junior finds most fulfilling...

World to Fantasy

A country girl, Maple, arrives to the big city, bewildered by the soaring skyscrapers and bustling streets... as well as her new roommate, some sort of shut-in indie voice actor who refuses to do anything fun outside... Maple's world is turned inside out when she finds that it's a lot larger... or smaller than she thinks when she's given an arcane quest between fantasy worlds to teach an unusual doll what it means to live.
